Alfredo pasta

  • Time: About 40 minutes
  • Rating: 5

Ingridients for recipe: 200 grams of taliatele, 2 chicken files, 1/2 onion, 6 mushrooms, 1/5 cup of sour cream, 1/2 cup of milk, 1/4 mug of cheese, 1 garlic, 1 table spoon of butter.

Preparation

  1. Add salt to the meat
  2. Cut the meat into strips
  3. After pan frying add butter
  4. Add onion, garlic and mushrooms
  5. Add sour cream and mix it
